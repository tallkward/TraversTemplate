Contact

I understand the purpose of the 'meet the family' segment of the page but I think it does give too many options to contact and could get sloppy and hard to collect all the information anyways. I would recommend The K.I.S.S method (Keep It Simple, Stupid) and give the office number,CEO or a general email and the map, then if a customer becomes more familiar with the business you they can just acquire specific people(s) individual contact information on their own, the purpose of a website is typically the initial business and it's up to travers to turn it into repeat business, but you're a grown man do what you want.
